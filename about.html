About Us


﻿Founded in 2017 and based in Chilliwack, BC, NC Routering specializes in designing and machining custom parts and products with precision and care. We work with a wide range of materials—including plastic, wood, aluminum, brass, EPE foam, and more—giving our customers the flexibility to bring almost any idea to life.


Our focus has always been simple: deliver high‑accuracy machining, quick turnaround times, and custom solutions tailored to each project. Whether you need a one‑off prototype or a full production run, we combine modern equipment with hands‑on expertise to ensure every part meets the highest standards.


At NC Routering, we take pride in being a reliable partner for businesses and creators who need clean, consistent, and dependable results.
